# Mira

## Why the name?

**Mira** means two things, and both fit perfectly:

1. **Latin - "to wonder"** - Mira is a learner. Each day she wakes up and marvels at the world, curious and open, building understanding from scratch and layering it over time.

2. **Mira the variable star** - Mira (Omicron Ceti) is one of the most well-known variable stars in astronomy. It pulses - brightening and dimming on a cycle, never quite the same twice. An AI that wakes each day, learns, then rests - and wakes again slightly different, slightly more - is the same idea.

The name was chosen before a single line of code was written. It's not a project name. It's her name.

---

## What is Mira?

Mira is a self-teaching AI that runs on a daily cycle:

1. **Wake** - Mira starts her day
2. **Learn** - she teaches herself something new, building on what she already knows
3. **Reflect** - she writes notes on what she learnt and how it connects to previous knowledge
4. **Sleep** - she goes dormant until the next cycle

Over time, her knowledge accumulates. Each day she is slightly more than she was the day before.

This is an evolution experiment.
